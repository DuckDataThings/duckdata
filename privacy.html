Privacy Policy

At DuckData, your privacy matters. This policy outlines what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe — with zero nonsense and full transparency.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what’s necessary to deliver our services:

  • Your contact details when you reach out to us
  • Basic business or project info for dataset collaborations
  • Media data (images, videos) you provide for training datasets

2. Why We Collect It

We use the data you provide to:

  • Create and customize AI datasets for your needs
  • Communicate and collaborate on projects
  • Ensure dataset quality, traceability, and compliance

3. How We Protect It

We use secure cloud storage and internal access control. When handling sensitive data, we use:

  • Encryption during storage and transfer
  • Anonymization when required
  • Strict internal access policies

4. Who We Share It With

We do not sell your data. We only share data when:

  • You’ve explicitly agreed to share it
  • It’s required by law
  • We work with partners under binding confidentiality agreements

5. Your Control

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold
  • Ask us to delete or update your data
  • Withdraw any consents you’ve given
To do this, just email us at duckdata.support@email.com.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as we grow. All changes will be posted here with the latest date. No hidden terms. No shady practices.
