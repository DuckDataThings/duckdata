About Us

DuckData was started by a group of passionate engineers who share a common vision for the future of artificial intelligence. With the goal of making AI more accessible and effective, DuckData works in close collaboration with researchers and companies around the world. We specialize in providing custom-built datasets to power machine learning systems across various industries. From traffic detection to gesture recognition, our data supports real-world AI applications.

The foundation of DuckData is built on the idea that smart data is the backbone of any intelligent system. We believe in precision, quality, and adaptability. With that in mind, our team works round the clock to collect, process, and deliver the exact datasets our clients require.

DuckData was founded by Mr. Chethan Kumar, an engineering visionary committed to bridging the gap between industry needs and advanced AI research. His leadership continues to guide the company in its mission to innovate and enable the next generation of data-driven solutions.
